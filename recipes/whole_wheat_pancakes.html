Whole wheat pancakes

Description

These 100% Whole Wheat Pancakes are a delightful choice for a healthy breakfast. The recipe is straightforward and yields delicious results. The pancakes are fluffy and can be topped with honey, peanut butter, jam, nuts, or fruits for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar or a packet of stevia
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix the egg with the flour, sugar, baking powder, and vanilla extract.
  2. If the mixture is too dry, add a splash of water.
  3. In a hot pan over high heat, pour a small amount of the mixture (keep in mind they are small pancakes). Flip them when bubbles start to appear on the top.
  4. For toppings, you can add honey, peanut butter, jam, nuts, or fruits.

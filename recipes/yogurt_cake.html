Yogurt cake

Description

This fluffy, moist, and delicious cake is prepared quickly. I decided to use some ripe bananas that were left in the fridge for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • 5 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 bananas
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Splash of vanilla extract
  • 1 pot of yogurt (120g)
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • Handful of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mash the bananas and mix with the tablespoon of honey and the vanilla extract. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs well. Continue beating and add the oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, yogurt, and cream cheese.
  3. Gradually add the flour to the mixture, alternating with the mashed bananas. Finally, fold in the chocolate chips, reserving some to sprinkle on top.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased mold, sprinkle some chocolate chips on top, and bake in a preheated oven at a gentle temperature of 170°C (340°F) until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let it cool slightly in the mold, then transfer to a cooling rack to cool completely.
  5. Very fluffy and light, delicious for sharing with family!
